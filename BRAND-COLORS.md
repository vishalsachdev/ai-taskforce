# UIUC Brand Colors Reference

This website uses official University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign brand colors per the [official brand guidelines](https://brand.illinois.edu/visual-identity/color).

---

## Primary Colors

### Illini Orange
<div style="background: #FF5F05; color: white; padding: 2rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <strong>#FF5F05</strong><br>
  RGB: 255/95/5<br>
  PMS: 1655
</div>

**Usage**: Central to our brand identity. Used for buttons, links, accents, and call-to-action elements.

---

### Illini Blue
<div style="background: #13294B; color: white; padding: 2rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <strong>#13294B</strong><br>
  RGB: 19/41/75<br>
  PMS: 2767
</div>

**Usage**: Primary color for headings, table headers, and major text elements. Should be used equally with orange when possible.

---

## Accessible Alternative

### Altgeld Orange
<div style="background: #C84113; color: white; padding: 2rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <strong>#C84113</strong><br>
  RGB: 200/65/19
</div>

**Usage**: Accessible option when Illini Orange does not provide enough color contrast. Used for button hover states and when accessibility requires higher contrast.

---

These should be used minimally as necessary or in applications where it's not possible to use only Illini orange or Illini blue.

</div>

## Supporting Colors

  <strong>#009FD4</strong> | RGB: 0/159/212
</div>

**Usage**: Highlight boxes and informational callouts.

## Application in This Site

### Navigation Cards
- **Border**: Storm Gray Light (#C8C6C7)
- **Hover Border**: Illini Orange (#FF5F05)
- **Hover Shadow**: Orange with 15% opacity
- **Headings**: Illini Blue (#13294B)
- **Buttons**: Illini Orange background, white text

### Tables
- **Header Background**: Illini Blue (#13294B)
- **Header Text**: White
- **Borders**: Storm Gray Light (#C8C6C7)
- **Hover Row**: Orange tint (5% opacity)

### Links
- **Default**: Illini Orange (#FF5F05)
- **Hover**: Altgeld Orange (#C84113)

### Blockquotes/Callouts
- **Background**: Orange tint (5% opacity)
- **Border**: Illini Orange (#FF5F05)
- **Text**: Illini Blue (#13294B)

### Code
- **Block Background**: Illini Blue (#13294B)
- **Block Text**: White
- **Inline Background**: Orange tint (10% opacity)
- **Inline Text**: Illini Blue (#13294B)
